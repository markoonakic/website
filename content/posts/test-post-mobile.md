Mobile-first design ensures your website works well on all devices, starting with the smallest screens.

## Why Mobile-First?

Most users access websites on mobile devices, so it makes sense to prioritize mobile experience.

## Key Principles

- Start with mobile layout
- Use responsive breakpoints
- Test on real devices

## Best Practices

Always test your designs on actual mobile devices to ensure they work correctly.
