Learn how to create websites that look great on all devices, from mobile phones to desktop computers.

## Mobile-First Approach

Starting with mobile designs and scaling up ensures your site works well on all screen sizes.

## Key Techniques

- Media queries
- Flexible units (rem, em, %)
- Flexible images

## Testing

Always test on real devices when possible, not just browser dev tools.
